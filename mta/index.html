Lately on the MTA

Every day in New York City, people ride public transit millions of times.

This website is automatically updated daily with the most recent available subway and bus data collected by the MTA. Full datasets are available at ny.data.gov.

Show info on site and data:

Data displayed on this site (including text and graphs) are calculated and cached daily from the most recent available transportation data on data.ny.gov.

Data was most recently cached on at GMT. View raw data cache

This site uses data from the following datasets:
- MTA Daily Ridership Data: Beginning 2020
- MTA Subway Hourly Ridership: Beginning February 2022
- MTA Bus Hourly Ridership: Beginning February 2022

Data may not be available for all stations and routes, and may not be continuous over all periods of time. Ridership data is based on estimates and may differ between datasets.

Source code for this site and the auto data cache / processing script are available on Github.
