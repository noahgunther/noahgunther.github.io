VRJam

Updated Dec 1, 2022



"VRJam" (working title) is an ongoing Unity project targeting Oculus Quest devices. In VRJam, players meet in groups and compete by playing through a series of random, 10-second microgames that utilize a wide variety of different fun VR interactions, such as launching a slingshot, blocking a shot on goal, firing a cannon, and more. Networking is made possible using the Photon Fusion networking toolset for Unity, with realtime speech using Photon Voice. Avatar mouths move to match speech using the Oculus VR lip sync assets. All other code was written by me in C# for Unity.
